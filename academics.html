About Us

Students coming to Rwanda Coding Academy are selected by Rwanda Education Board througn the normal process of student placement. Rwanda Coding Academy is listed amongst the schools students leaving Ordinary Level can choose from, and after the publication of National Examen Results Rwanda Education Board selects those who chose to study at rwanda Coding Academy and who qualified are qualified for that.
